About Me

I'm a passionate product designer dedicated to crafting digital experiences that blend
creativity and functionality. Currently pursuing a Master’s in Information Science at the
University of Michigan, I’m focused on exploring the intersection of design, technology, and innovation.
With experience across Ecommerce, Entertainment, Finance, Dining, and Education sectors,
I’ve delivered impactful projects in both B2C and B2B (SaaS) spaces. I specialize in interactive
animations and customer-centric design, leveraging various design tools to creatively solve complex problems.
Outside of design, I enjoy sketching, photography, writing, singing, traveling,
and playing the ukulele, all of which contribute to a well-rounded skill set and strong interpersonal abilities.
Let’s explore design thinking, user empathy, and the endless possibilities
of creating meaningful, resonant experiences together.
